About Me

I’m Saikomal Reddypally, a Computer Science student passionate about blending tradition with modern technology. My journey began in 11th grade with Python and SQL, sparking a love for solving real-world problems through data. As a Big Data Analytics student, I explore how AI and data can power smart, human-centered solutions.

I’ve built projects like a hand gesture control system and ‘Swipe Movies,’ using the MEAN stack. My toolkit includes Python, SQL, Power BI, and machine learning fundamentals.

Beyond code, I’m a Brown Belt (First Q) in Karate with National and Inter-State silver medals, trained in focus and resilience. Creativity is my second nature—I tell stories through video editing, photography, and cinematography.

In every line of code or creative frame, I aim to build experiences that inspire, solve, and connect.
